    About

    About Kolej Siswa Jaya

    Kolej Siswa Jaya started pioneering student admission for the first time on June 1, 2003, the 2003/2004 session with a capacity of 3000 students. Block A and block B level 1 house students while students are placed in block C and block B on level 2 and above. There are 7 floors in each block and each room accommodates 2 occupants only.

    UTM Kuala Lumpur Student College Kuala Lumpur is located at Jalan Rejang 4, Setapak Jaya, Kuala Lumpur which is about 3KM from the UTM Kuala Lumpur campus. With an area of 3.24 hectares, this college was built at a cost of RM76 million.

    Why stay at Kolej Siswa Jaya?

    Despite our shortcomings, Kolej Siswa Jaya is a pleasant place to stay.

    • 01 Free bus service

      Traveling just became a whole lot easier with our complimentary bus. Enjoy a free ride to the University of Technology, Kuala Lumpur or LRT Dato' Keramat and don’t worry about finding spare change for parking.

    • 02 Easy access to Kuala Lumpur

      Imagine waking up and taking refreshing jog or enjoying a romantic sunset stroll with your loved one along the bay. When you stay at Kolej Siswa Jaya, you can walk directly across the street to beautiful Greater Kuala Lumpur.

    • 03 Fast and Free Internet

      We understand the importance of sharing your travel moments on Instagram or Facebook, checking your emails, streaming your favorite playlist from Pandora, and all that jazz. We offer lightning speed Internet and Wi-Fi access for free.

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I apply to stay at Kolej Siswa Jaya?

    To apply for residency, please sign up regardless of your relation with UTM.

    How do I apply for vehicle sticker?

    To apply for vehicle sticker, please login into KSJConnects. You can find the button at the top of the page.

    Are diploma students/degree students required to stay at Kolej Siswa Jaya for the first year

    Yes. Unless provided with good reason.

    How do I re-apply to Kolej Siswa Jaya as a current UTM student?

    The basic criteria is to have enough merit points to compete with other student. Seniority does not play a role.

    Does the merit system apply to residents who are not from UTM?

    No.
